1 Set up your iOS Shortcut

Tap below to copy this app's URL and open Shortcuts, then:

  • Select "Automation" (bottom‑middle tab)
  • Tap "+" → "Create Personal Automation"
  • Select "App" from the list
  • Choose your social media apps → "Is Opened" → "Run Immediately"
  • Add the action "Open URL" and paste the copied URL
  • Save — done!
